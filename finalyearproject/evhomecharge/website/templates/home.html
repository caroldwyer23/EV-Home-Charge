Range Anxiety Reduction

Knowing that a charging station is available at to rent locally at a time that suits you can help alleviate range anxiety.

Pre-booking an EV charging session can help ensure that a charging station is available when you need it, which can save time and hassle compared to searching for an available charging station. This can also help you plan your trip or schedule, ensuring that you have enough charge to reach your destination or the next charging station on your route.

Support EV Adaption

By offering to rent your EV charge point with others, you are helping to support the adoption of EVs by making it easier for others to charge their vehicles. This can contribute to a cleaner and more sustainable transportation future.

Cost Saving

If you charge a fee for using your EV charge point, sharing it can help offset the cost of installation and maintenance. Additionally, if you share the cost of electricity with others, you can reduce your own charging expenses.

If you don't use your EV charge point all the time, sharing it with others can help put an underutilized asset to good use. By sharing, you can help others and earn some extra money at the same time.
