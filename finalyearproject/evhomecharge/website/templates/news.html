How many electric vehicle charging connectors are available in Ireland?

In Ireland, there are typically three types of electric vehicle charging connectors:

Type 2 Connector (Mennekes Connector): This is the most common type of connector in Ireland and is used for both AC and DC charging. It has seven pins and is capable of providing up to 43 kW of power.

CHAdeMO Connector: This connector is primarily used for DC fast charging and is capable of providing up to 50 kW of power. It is less common than the Type 2 connector in Ireland.

CCS Connector (Combo Connector): This connector is also primarily used for DC fast charging and is capable of providing up to 350 kW of power. It combines the Type 2 connector with two additional DC pins, allowing it to provide both AC and DC charging. This connector is becoming increasingly common in Ireland.

It's worth noting that there may be other types of connectors available at specific charging stations, such as Tesla's proprietary connector, but the three types listed above are the most commonly used in Ireland.
